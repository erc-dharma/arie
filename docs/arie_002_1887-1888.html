APPENDIX II.



Notes on copper-plate Grants in the possession of the Srirangam Temple.



ARIE/1887-1888/Notes on copper-plate Grants in the possession of the Srirangam Temple//1. (page 6)
Place
A grant of three plates. The seal contains a boar, the sun and the moon. The grant was issued during the reign of Krishnaraya of Vijayanagara in Saka 1436, Bhava.
ARIE/1887-1888/Notes on copper-plate Grants in the possession of the Srirangam Temple//2. (page 6)
Place
A grant of five plates with ring, but without seal. It belongs to the reign of Viradeva-raya.
ARIE/1887-1888/Notes on copper-plate Grants in the possession of the Srirangam Temple//3. (page 6)
Place
Three plates without seal. Devaraya of Vijayanagara, Saka 1356, Ananda.
ARIE/1887-1888/Notes on copper-plate Grants in the possession of the Srirangam Temple//4. (page 6)
Place
Three plates. Seal with boar, sun and moon. Krishnaraya of Vijayanagara, Saka 1450 (?) Sarvajit.
ARIE/1887-1888/Notes on copper-plate Grants in the possession of the Srirangam Temple//5. (page 6)
Place
